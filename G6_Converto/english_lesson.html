English System Converter

The English system, also known as the Imperial system, uses the foot (12 inches) as the main unit of length and the pound (16 ounces) as the primary unit of weight. Despite the UK's shift to the metric system, the Imperial system is still used for specific purposes such as personal measurements and road distances.
