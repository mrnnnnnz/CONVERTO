Metric System Converter

The metric system is a decimal-based system of measurement widely used across the globe. It simplifies conversions between units by employing powers of 10, making calculations and comparisons straightforward. Its base units include the meter for length, kilogram for mass, and second for time, forming a coherent and versatile framework for scientific and everyday measurements.
